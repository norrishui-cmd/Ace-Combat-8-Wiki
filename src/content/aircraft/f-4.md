## How to unlock it

Unlocked through the in-game **Aircraft Tree** during normal progression, same as any other base-game aircraft — every edition gets the aircraft itself.

The **Mobius Squadron 25th Anniversary livery** is part of the Deluxe Edition's Customisable Cosmetic Set and is a skin-only bonus. It's a nod to Mobius One and the legendary squadron from earlier Ace Combat entries, but doesn't affect whether you get the F-4 itself.
